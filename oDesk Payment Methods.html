Foreign Status Certification

FOREIGN CONTRACTORS
(W-8BEN CERTIFICATION)

The U.S. government generally subjects foreign people to United States tax at a rate of 30 percent on income they receive from domestic sources. If you are not a U.S. citizen and wish to receive your full payment, you must make the following certification:

I declare, under penalty of perjury, that I am exempt from U.S. tax withholding because:

I am not a U.S. citizen; and

I performed, or will perform, all services outside of the United States for which I receive payment through oDesk.

I further certify that I have read, and I meet, the tax exemptions conditions in the Certificate of Foreign Status of Beneficial Owner for United States Tax Withholding.

Form W-8BEN (Rev. February 2006)

See IRS Notice 2001-4 http://www.irs.gov/businesses/small/international/article/0,,id=106263,00.html

WE CAN NOT PROVIDE YOU WITH TAX ADVICE. IF YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS OR CAN NOT DETERMINE IF YOU ARE EXEMPT FROM TAX WITHHOLDING, WE URGE YOU TO CONSULT A TAX PROFESSIONAL OR OTHER EXPERT TO ASSIST YOU.

美国政府通常收取外籍人士从其本国国内来源获得的收入的30％作为税收。如果您不是美国公民，并希望收到您的全额付款，则必须作出以下认证：

我宣布，根据伪证处罚，我受美国预扣税豁免，因为：

我进一步证明我已看过，和我见面，在美国预扣税的实益拥有外籍身份证明书免税条件。
